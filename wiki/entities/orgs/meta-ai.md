# Meta AI (FAIR)

## Type

Industry research lab — Meta Fundamental AI Research. Distinct from
[[meta-reality-labs]] (AR/VR-focused).

## Focus areas relevant to this wiki

Computer vision foundation models (DINO, DINOv2, DINOv3, SAM, V-JEPA),
multimodal models, video understanding, point tracking, 3D
reconstruction. Frequent partner of [[oxford-vgg]] through joint PhD
students.
