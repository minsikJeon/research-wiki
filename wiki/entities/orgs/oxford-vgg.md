# Visual Geometry Group, University of Oxford

## Type

Academic research group at University of Oxford. Led historically by
Andrew Zisserman; current PIs include [[andrea-vedaldi]],
[[christian-rupprecht]], Joao Carreira, and others.

## Focus areas relevant to this wiki

Classical computer vision continuity (SfM, multi-view geometry) merged
with modern deep learning. Strong on video understanding, point tracking,
3D / 4D reconstruction (DUSt3R, MASt3R, VGGSfM, VGGT, V-DPM), self-
supervised representation learning.
