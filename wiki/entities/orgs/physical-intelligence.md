# Physical Intelligence (π)

## Type

Industry research lab focused on **robot foundation models**. Founded
2024 by Sergey Levine, Chelsea Finn, Karol Hausman, and Brian Ichter
(among others). Based in San Francisco, with heavy collaboration with
UC Berkeley.

## Focus areas relevant to this wiki

- **VLAs** for dexterous bimanual manipulation: π0 (2024), π0.5 (2024).
- **Flow-matching policies** for action chunking.
- **Real-time execution** of large policies — see [[rtc]].

The shorthand "PI" in robotics circles refers to this group; not to be
confused with other PI acronyms.
