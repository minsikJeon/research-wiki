# NVIDIA

## Type

Industrial research lab. NVIDIA Research / NVIDIA AI. Large CV +
robotics group with substantial publication output in feedforward 3D
reconstruction, autonomous driving perception, foundation models.

## Focus areas relevant to this wiki

3D scene reconstruction at scale, multi-view geometry, foundation
models for vision, autonomous driving stack, embodied AI,
manipulation, and Isaac robotics.

## Sources from this org

- [[elflein-2026-vgg-t3]] — VGG-T3 (NVIDIA + Vector Institute +
  U. Toronto). Scalable feed-forward 3D reconstruction via TTT-based
  KV compression.

## Notes

NVIDIA research papers on feedforward 3D reconstruction tend to
emphasize **scale and efficiency** (compatible with their hardware
agenda). VGG-T3's 1-GPU-handles-2k-images claim and clean DDP scaling
are characteristic.

Other NVIDIA Research vision groups to watch as new papers arrive:
Isaac Sim / robotic manipulation (Dieter Fox, Yashraj Narang), autonomous
driving perception (Sanja Fidler's Toronto AI group is closely
affiliated via Vector Institute), generative video / world models.
