# Adam W. Harley

## Affiliation

Stanford University (current). Prior CMU PhD with [[katerina-fragkiadaki]].

## Main contributions (within this wiki)

- **[[tapip3d]]** (NeurIPS 2025) — co-author on the world-coord 3D point
  tracker.

His broader contributions to TAP (cited extensively across this wiki's
sources but not yet ingested):
- **PIPs** (NeurIPS 2022) — Particle Video Revisited; the modern
  deep-learning TAP architecture that every subsequent method builds on
  or contrasts with.
