# Adam W. Harley

## Affiliation

Stanford University (current). Prior CMU PhD with
[[katerina-fragkiadaki]] — both [[harley-2022-pips]] and [[tapip3d]]
trace back to that group.

## Main contributions (within this wiki)

- **[[harley-2022-pips]]** (ECCV 2022) — Particle Video Revisited;
  the foundational deep-learning TAP architecture. 8-frame MLP-Mixer
  iterative refinement over correlation pyramids; independent
  per-point tracking; FlyingThings++ synthetic-with-injected-occluders
  training recipe. Every modern TAP method in this wiki cites or
  extends this work.
- **[[tapip3d]]** (NeurIPS 2025) — co-author on the world-coord 3D
  point tracker; the architectural successor in 3D.
- **PIPs++** — long-term tracking extension. *Not yet ingested.*
