# Sergey Levine

## Affiliation

Professor at [[uc-berkeley]] (BAIR) + co-founder of
[[physical-intelligence]]. One of the most prolific researchers in
robot learning over the past decade — RL, imitation learning, offline
RL, robot foundation models.

## Main contributions (within this wiki)

- **[[act]]** (2023, [[zhao-2023-act]]) — co-author with Zhao, Kumar,
  Finn. Founding action chunking paper.
- **[[rtc]]** (2025, [[black-2025-rtc]]) — senior author. Real-time
  chunking via inference-time inpainting.
- **[[training-time-rtc]]** (2025, [[black-2025-training-time-rtc]]) —
  senior author. Training-time prefix conditioning replacement for RTC.

Broader research line **not yet in this wiki** (likely to grow fast as
more robotics papers are ingested):
- Deep RL for manipulation (early work)
- Offline RL (CQL, IQL, etc.)
- π0, π0.5, π0.6, OpenVLA (with collaborators), Octo, RT-X
- Robot foundation models, generalist manipulation
