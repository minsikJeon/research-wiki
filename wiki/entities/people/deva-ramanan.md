# Deva Ramanan

## Affiliation

[[cmu-ri]] — Professor at the Robotics Institute, also at Argo AI
historically. **User's school** — likely a top-priority faculty for the

## Main contributions (within this wiki)

Three sources, spanning a decade — a long research arc connecting his
2020 foundational work on streaming perception with the current
"Anything" feed-forward 3D/4D line at CMU:

- **[[li-2020-streaming-perception]]** (ECCV 2020) — senior author on
  the foundational streaming-accuracy paper. Established that *tracking
  and forecasting emerge as necessary* for embodied perception.
- **[[mapanything]]** (2025) — co-senior author with Sebastian Scherer.
- **[[any4d]]** (2025) — co-senior author.

The arc is meaningful: the 2020 paper's "tracking + forecasting are
necessary internal representations" finding pre-dates and *parallels*
the [[q-emergent-tracking-heuristics]] thread from the TAPNext
discussion. His current 4D-reconstruction line (MapAnything, Any4D) is
the modern continuation of the embodied-perception agenda the 2020
paper opened.

His broader research line (much not yet in this wiki): video object
tracking, autonomous driving perception (Argo AI affiliation), dynamic
scene understanding, long-tailed recognition. One of the most prolific
CV researchers at CMU; expect heavy future representation in this wiki.
