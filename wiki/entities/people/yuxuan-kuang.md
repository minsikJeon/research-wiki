## Notes

Track record in dexterous manipulation:
- **StopNet** (ICRA 2024, Dex4D ref [18]) — multiview 6-DoF suction
  detection.
- **RAM** (Kuang et al., 2024, Dex4D ref [19]) — retrieval-based
  affordance transfer for zero-shot robotic manipulation.
- **SkillBlender** (2025, Dex4D ref [20]) — versatile humanoid
  whole-body loco-manipulation via skill blending.
- **FetchBot** (CoRL 2025 — Dex4D ref [26]) — generalizable object
  fetching in cluttered scenes via zero-shot sim2real.

This is the Tulsiani × Fragkiadaki first-author of Dex4D, the second
major source in this wiki's CMU manipulation-via-point-tracks line
(after [[track2act]]).
