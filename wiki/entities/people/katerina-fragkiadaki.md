# Katerina Fragkiadaki

## Affiliation

[[cmu-ri]] — Associate Professor at Carnegie Mellon University's Machine
Learning Department + Robotics Institute. **User's institution.**

## Main contributions (within this wiki)

- **[[harley-2022-pips]]** (ECCV 2022) — senior author on the founding
  deep-learning TAP method. The user's institution is the origin point
  of the modern TAP sub-field.
- **[[tapip3d]]** (NeurIPS 2025) — senior author on the world-coord 3D
  point tracking paper.
- **[[dex4d]]** (2026) — **co-senior** with [[shubham-tulsiani]] on
  the sim-to-real dexterous-manipulation-from-point-tracks paper.
  First authors Yuxuan Kuang + Sungjae Park. This connects the
  Fragkiadaki TAP line to the [[point-tracks-as-manipulation-interface]]
  family, downstream of [[harley-2022-pips]] / [[zhang-2025-tapip3d]].

Her broader research line (not all in this wiki yet): embodied AI,
spatial reasoning, vision-language-action models, video-language
understanding.
