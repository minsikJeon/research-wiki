# Görkay Aydemir

## Affiliation

Koç University, Istanbul. Supervised jointly by Weidi Xie (Shanghai Jiao
Tong) and Fatma Güney (Koç) per [[aydemir-2025-track-on2]].

## Main contributions (within this wiki)

The Track-On line — memory-based causal point trackers:

- **Track-On** (ICLR 2025) — original dual-memory online tracker. Not
  yet ingested as its own source; referenced from
  [[aydemir-2025-track-on2]].
- **[[track-on2]]** (2025, IEEE TPAMI submission) — successor with
  single expandable memory, multi-scale ViT-Adapter features, and
  DINOv2/v3 backbones.

## Notes

The classification-first localization formulation is Aydemir's signature
contribution — predates and is conceptually adjacent to [[tapnext]]'s
classification coordinate head. Worth tracking for future memory-based
online tracker work.
